# Agency Setup Guide

<Accordion title="Understanding Agencies in AI Services">
An agency is a business entity that provides specialized services to clients. In the context of our AI platform, an agency offers AI-driven services such as chatbots, natural language processing, automated customer service systems, and other AI-powered tools.

GlowStudio enables you to create and manage your AI services, client relationships, and brand presence with ease. By setting up your agency in GlowStudio, you gain access to powerful tools for customization, client management, and service delivery.
</Accordion>

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Setting Up Your Agency: A Comprehensive Guide">
Follow these detailed steps to set up and optimize your agency on our AI platform:

<Steps>

<Step title="Configure the Overview Tab">
The Overview tab is your agency's command center. Here's what you need to do:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set your agency's name">
    Choose a name that reflects your brand identity and the AI services you offer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define your branding text (URL)">
    This will be part of your agency's unique URL on our platform. Make it concise and memorable.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate a secret key">
    This key provides secure access to all your agency's widgets. Keep it confidential.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload your agency logo">
    Ensure it's a high-quality image that represents your brand well. Recommended size: 200x200 pixels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your secret key is crucial for security. You can regenerate it at any time by clicking the "Regen Secret" button in the Overview tab. It's recommended to rotate this key periodically for enhanced security.
</Info>
</Step>

</Steps>
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
