# Customize Your Agency's Theme
Make your agency stand out with a unique visual identity:

In the Theme tab, select your desired brand colors and theme variant. The colors are highly customizable.
Choose fonts that match your brand and customize button styles, form elements, and other UI components to fit your vision.
Use the Custom CSS editor to fine-tune your Client Dashboard appearance.


 ```CSS Example Custom CSS
  .dashboard-header{
    background-color: #003366;
    color: #ffffff;
  }
  .client-name {
    font-family: Roboto, sans-serif;font-weight: 700;
    }
  ```
<Check>
After customizing, preview your agency's appearance across different sections to ensure a consistent and professional look.
</Check>
